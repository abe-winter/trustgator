trustgator

Link aggregator with 2-hop trust network over topics

⚠️ This is a demo -- use and enjoy it but remember it's a WIP/POC. It's heavily rate-limited and it may change or break.

Login Join trustgator github

Overview

The overall goal here is to understand how the people in your network view a particular piece of information.

Trust features

Assertions

Users can 'assert' claims about articles which are visible in the article's thread. Assertions have an attached topic badge so you may end up trusting a certain user on 'long haul trucking' but not on 'modern dance'.

Logged-in users will see activity from their trust network as an overlay on posted links

Vouches

Users can 'vouch' for assertions that interest them. Vouches can be positive but also neutral i.e. 'interesting but not sure' or negative i.e. 'disagree'. The goal isn't to hide content you disagree with.

Surfaced articles

The articles on a user's homepage are those with recent assert/vouch activity from their 2-hop network (i.e. users you've vouched and users your vouchees have vouched)
